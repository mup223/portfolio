After School Programs in Denver

Click anywhere on this map and all of the points of interest within 1 km of that lat/long will be highlighted. The data was acquired by Denver Open Data Catalog. This map is an illustraion of how to make multiple leaflet layers interactive.

Poplation Density for Three Cities

This lab will use your JavaScript functions to accept user input and calculate population density measures for three cities in two different units. You will store your city information as arrays and will iterate through them and use functions to calculate density.

Choropleth Map of Rent in US

This map shows the average rent in every county in the US. Data obtained through the American Fact Finder (factfinder.census.gov); 2010 Census, SF1 joined to Census Cartographic Boundary Shapefiles (www.census.gov/geography) using QGIS.
